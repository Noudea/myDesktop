About us

Who are we?

A love for all things buttercream and our very own @Cocobunbuns, Canberra's most famous bunny.

What do we do?

Specialing in buttercream craft, we are known for our intricate hand pipped flowers, bespoke cakes, and special sweet packages.

How we do it

We source only the freshest Australian, in season produce to flavour our creations. Baked fresh and to order, every bite is a guaranteed mouthfull of goodness!
